Margherita

Usually this pizza made with basil, mozzarella, cheese and tomatoes in imitation of the colors of the italian's lag.

Marinara

This is a traditional Neapolitan pizza that is made with lots of garlic, anchovies and oregano.

Capricciosa

This pizza is topped of olives, artichoke heards, half of boilled egg, prosciuto and mushrooms.

Sfincione

This is a thick sheet pizza that is made in Sicily and uses anchovies, caciocavallo, breadcrumbs, and tomato sauce.

Quattro Formagi

This is made using a four cheese combination of mozzarella, parmiiano-reggiano, ricotta and Gorgonzola.

Romana

This is a pizza that contains anchovies, cappery, mozzarella and tomatoes.
